Campus life

Assembly Area

The day begins with a morning assembly. A value-based morning assembly emphasizes the School’s secular approach and influences students to understand and enjoy India’s rich heritage and culture. It reinforces ethics, etiquette, values and strengthens moral character.

Library

The text books and notebooks are provided by the School and kept in the School premises. These are not taken home except with the permission of the Teachers or Principal.Further more it has been found that the lesson in the class and the self-study periods are usually adequate for the purposes of learning and understanding.

Nath valley clubs

The clubs of our school have lived up to the school’s mottos-Learning with Pleasure, Excellence in education, Nothing but the best, World citizens.Extra curriculum especially in the form of clubs is focused on bringing about the complete development of a child’s personality.
